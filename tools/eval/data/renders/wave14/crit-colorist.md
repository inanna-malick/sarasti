### THUMBNAIL CLUSTER TEST (64x64)
*   **Zone: RED ALERT** (`full_crisis`, `aggressive_1.0`, `cross_wired-aggressive`, `cross_smirk-aggressive`)
    *   *Result:* Immediate "High Action" signal. The pink-red shift makes this look more like blood/physiology and less like a filter.
*   **Zone: VITALITY ROSY** (`euphoric_1.0`, `yielding_1.0`, `cross_euphoric-yielding`)
    *   *Result:* Success/Positive signal. The removal of the "orange" makes these feel much more natural and trustworthy.
*   **Zone: BASELINE BEIGE** (`neutral`, `full_calm`, `smirk_1.0`, `full_puffy-soyboi-calm`)
    *   *Result:* Stable/Inactive signal. **Crucially, `alarmed_1.0` has exited this cluster.**
*   **Zone: ANXIOUS PALE** (`alarmed_1.0`, `wired_1.0`, `cross_alarmed-wired`, `cross_smirk-alarmed`)
    *   *Result:* High-frequency stress signal. The pallor is now a distinct color state that is readable at thumbnail.
*   **Zone: DEPLETED GRAY** (`exhausted_1.0`, `cross_exhausted-yielding`, `cross_euphoric-exhausted`)
    *   *Result:* Failure/Inactive signal. Maximum contrast to the "Red Alert" zone.

### COMPARISON: Wave 14 vs Wave 13
*   **`alarmed_1.0`**: **FIX SUCCESSFUL.** The doubled pallor strength has transformed this from "worried neutral" to "physiologically shocked." It now communicates fear through color alone, even before the expression is processed.
*   **`euphoric_1.0`**: **FIX SUCCESSFUL.** The shift from orange to pink-red has eliminated the "fake tan" look. It now looks like a healthy, oxygenated flush.
*   **`full_crisis`**: **IMPROVED.** The deeper pink-red feels more visceral and medical, reinforcing the "physiological overload" better than the previous orange-heavy red.

### REMAINING PROBLEMS
*   **`full_sharp-chad-crisis`**: Still leans slightly too "golden/heroic." Given it's a "crisis" state, it could afford to lean 10-15% more into the pink-red of `full_crisis` to show more strain.
*   **`cross_smirk-alarmed`**: While better (cooler), this remains the "muddiest" color state. It sits in a gray-beige middle ground that is slightly less communicative than the purer states.
