### CONTRAST MAP: Most Distinguishable Pairs
These pairs provide the highest visual delta for financial dashboards where quick recognition is key:
*   **`full_crisis` vs `exhausted_1.0`**: Maximum Red vs Maximum Gray. Absolute "Active" vs "Dead" contrast.
*   **`aggressive_1.0` vs `wired_1.0`**: Body Heat vs Electric Pallor.
*   **`euphoric_1.0` vs `cross_exhausted-yielding`**: Golden Success vs Blue-Gray Defeat.

### PROBLEM FACES: Color vs. Expression Conflict
*   **`alarmed_1.0`**: The expression screams fear, but the skin tone is too healthy. A "Senior Colorist" would pull the reds and oranges out of the highlights to simulate blood draining from the face.
*   **`cross_smirk-alarmed`**: This face looks "flat" because the color doesn't lean into the panic or the ego. It should probably be cooler to highlight the "nervous" part of the alarm.

### THUMBNAIL TEST: Cluster Analysis (64x64)
At small sizes, these faces will cluster into these "color zones":

*   **Zone: RED ALERT** (`full_crisis`, `aggressive_1.0`, `cross_wired-aggressive`)
    *   *Result:* Looks like a "Hot/Warning" indicator. Very distinguishable from other zones.
*   **Zone: GOLDEN GLOW** (`euphoric_1.0`, `yielding_1.0`, `cross_euphoric-yielding`)
    *   *Result:* Looks like "Safe/Positive" indicators.
