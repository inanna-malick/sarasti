# FLAME Expression Component Analysis (ψ20 - ψ29) - Multi-Angle Report

This report provides a detailed analysis of the FLAME expression components ψ20 through ψ29, incorporating observations from multiple camera angles (Front, Left 3/4, and Closeup).

---

## ψ20: Visceral Disgust / Lip Sneer
*   **Negative Pole (-3.0):**
    *   **Brow:** Neutral to slightly lowered.
    *   **Eyes:** Slightly narrowed from the bottom.
    *   **Mouth:** Upper lip is significantly pulled up and curled, creating a "sneer" or "pain" quality.
    *   **Jaw:** Neutral.
    *   **Cheeks:** Slightly raised/tensed.
    *   **Nasolabial Folds:** Significantly more pronounced, especially near nostrils; nostrils slightly flared.
    *   **Overall Impression:** Visceral disgust or reaction to sharp pain.
*   **Positive Pole (+3.0):**
    *   **Brow:** Neutral.
    *   **Eyes:** Neutral.
    *   **Mouth:** Upper lip is pulled down/elongated and smoothed; philtrum area is flattened.
    *   **Jaw:** Neutral.
    *   **Cheeks:** Smoothed/flattened.
    *   **Nasolabial Folds:** Nearly invisible, area is extremely smooth.
    *   **Overall Impression:** Stoic, suppressed emotion, or a "flat" expression.
*   **Angle Observations:** `left34` reveals the depth of the nasolabial fold and the upper lip protrusion much better than the front view. `closeup` confirms fine texture changes in the mid-face.
*   **Gradient Smoothness:** 5/5
*   **Label:** Visceral Disgust / Lip Sneer
*   **Artifacts:** None.
