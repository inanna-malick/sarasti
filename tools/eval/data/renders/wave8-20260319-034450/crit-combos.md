## Review Questions

### 1. Notable Faces
1. **shapexpr_sharp-wired-aggressive.png**: The extreme sharpness combined with "wired" intensity creates a predatory, high-contrast silhouette that pops.
2. **cross_alar_1.5_aggr_1.5.png**: The combination of max alarm and max aggression creates a "maniacal" facial tension that is impossible to ignore.
3. **shape_lean-patriarch.png**: The skeletal, vulture-like profile is unique among the more "fleshy" base meshes, creating a distinct visual "read."

### 2. Blurring Together
1. **shape_puffy-young.png**, **shape_puffy-soyboi.png**, **shape_puffy-chad.png**: These share very similar soft-tissue distributions on the same base topology; at 64px, they are just "round faces."
2. **cross_fati_-1.5_aggr_-1.5.png** and **neutral.png**: The lack of activation makes these practically identical at a glance.
3. **shape_sharp-young.png** and **shape_sharp-soyboi.png**: The sharpness modifier homogenizes the jawlines and cheekbones into a nearly identical "young-alpha" look.

### 3. Sick/Broken/Wrong
- **shape_bloated-intern.png**: The bloat causes some unnatural mesh bunching around the jawline/neck junction.
- **cross_alar_1.5_fati_1.5.png**: Stretching for alarm while sagging for fatigue creates an "uncanny melting" effect around the eyelids.
- **shape_sharp-old.png**: Looks like a "shrink-wrapped" skull; the lack of subcutaneous fat makes the "old" texture look like it's painted on a statue.

### 4. Fine vs. Wrong
- **Fine Pile (Decided in <1s)**: `neutral`, `shape_lean-patriarch`, `shape_puffy-old`, `cross_alar_-1.5_aggr_-1.5`. These have low facial tension and "regular" eye apertures.
- **Wrong Pile (Decided in <1s)**: `shapexpr_sharp-wired-aggressive`, `cross_alar_1.5_aggr_1.5`, `shapexpr_sharp-alarmed`, `cross_alar_1.5_fati_1.5`. These show extreme feature deformation—sclera exposure (alarm) or bared teeth (aggression)—that triggers a lizard-brain response.

### 5. Trader's Glance
- **The "Sclera Flash"**: Any face transitioning to a high-alarm state where the white of the eyes becomes prominent (like `shapexpr_sharp-alarmed`) acts like a red flashing light.
- **Mouth Contortion**: A sudden shift from a closed mouth to the "bared-teeth" snarl of aggression or the "slack-jaw" of exhaustion is an immediate indicator of high volatility in that instrument.
