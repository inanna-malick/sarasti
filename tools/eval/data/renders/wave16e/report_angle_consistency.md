# Wave16e Angle Consistency Report

## Executive Summary
The Wave16e facial expression set shows exceptional angle consistency. Previous issues with view-dependent illusions (like lateral jaw shifts that only worked from the front) appear to be fully resolved. The transition from Front to Left34 maintains emotional integrity and anatomical correctness.

## Key Findings
1. **Emotional Integrity:** Every expression communicates the same intent from the side as it does from the front. There is no "loss of emotion" during rotation.
2. **Mesh Stability:** No visible artifacts like asymmetric deformations, jaw shifting, or eye distortion were found in the 3/4 view.
3. **Rotation Readiness:** All expressions would survive a smooth camera rotation without "snapping" or looking broken. The 3D volumes are well-defined.

## Recommendation
Approved for production use. No further refinement for angle consistency is required for this wave.
