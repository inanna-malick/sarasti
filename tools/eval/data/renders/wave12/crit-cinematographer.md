### 2. Best Marriages (Expression + Color)
- **combo_crisis.png**: The extreme red flush perfectly communicates the "overheat" of a total crisis.
- **tex_cold_tired.png**: The desaturated, blue-ish tone is the exact color of "depletion."
- **combo_euphoric-yielding.png**: The warm "glow" makes the smile feel genuine rather than pasted on.
- **full_sharp-chad-aggro-smirk.png**: The "blooded" skin makes the aggression feel biological and dangerous.

### 3. Conflicting Expression and Color
- **combo_euphoric-exhausted.png**: A "manic" smile paired with "deathly" eyes. It creates cognitive dissonance—great for a psychological thriller, but confusing for a simple data read.
- **full_puffy-soyboi-exhausted-yielding.png**: The "soft" face shape usually suggests safety, but the "ill" sallow color suggests a victim.
- **expr_alarmed_1.0.png**: The "healthy" skin tone undermines the "alarm" in the eyes. A panicked person shouldn't look like they just had a spa day.

### 4. Thumbnail Visibility (64x64)
**Honestly? Yes.** At 64x64, fine facial details like "smirks" or "soft yields" will vanish. But the **color block** of the face—the difference between a "red-faced" crisis and a "blue-faced" exhaustion—will be the *only* thing that remains clear. Color is the low-frequency data that survives downsampling.

### 5. Recommendations for a Financial Crisis Dashboard
If I were grading these for a "Crisis" state:
- **Temperature:** Shift toward **Sallow-Cyan** for "Exhaustion/Market Stagnation" and **Abrasive Magenta-Red** for "Aggressive Crisis."
- **Saturation:** Desaturate the "Exhaustion" faces even further—make them look like paper. Over-saturate the "Crisis" faces in the "T-zone" (forehead and nose) to suggest high blood pressure.
- **Specific Changes:**
    - **Dark Circles:** Make them more "purple" (venous) rather than just "dark." This suggests lack of sleep from overnight trading.
    - **Highlight Sheen:** Add a "sweat" specular highlight to the "Aggressive/Crisis" faces. A matte red face is just a color; a *shiny* red face is a person in a meltdown.
    - **Contrast:** Increase local contrast around the eyes for "Alarmed" states to make the sclera (whites of the eyes) pop more, even at small sizes.
