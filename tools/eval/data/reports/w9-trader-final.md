## Technical Review

### If this was your only monitoring tool (no charts), could you trade with it?
Yes, but strictly as a **regime-detection tool**. It excels at identifying the "character" of the market (e.g., distinguishing between a healthy pullback vs. a depressed slow-bleed). It acts as a qualitative VIX, allowing a trader to "feel" the crowd's emotion. However, for precise execution (stop-loss placement, exact entry prices), traditional charts would still be necessary.

### What's the minimum number of faces where you'd want to see RED before hedging?
In a multi-asset environment, I would hedge when **3 or more faces** show the RED (C-style terror) signal, indicating systemic contagion. For a single-asset monitor, seeing **Face C** once is an immediate "sell first, ask questions later" trigger.

### Is there any face that gives a FALSE SIGNAL?
**Face J** is the most ambiguous. The wide-eyed "shock" expression could be interpreted as the start of a panic (RED) if the slight upturn in the mouth corners is missed. Similarly, **Face A** and **Face L** (Euphoria) could be misread as "Manic Distress" by an untrained eye, though the positive valence is clear upon closer inspection.
