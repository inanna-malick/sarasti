# psi18 — Right Mid-Face / Right Cheek (Conjugate: psi17)

## Description

Psi18 is the right-handed mirror of psi17 — the second member of the mid-face conjugate pair. Everything about psi17 applies here in mirror: the deformation is lateralized to the right side of the mid-face.

At positive values, the right cheek area tightens subtly and the right nasolabial fold adjusts. At negative values, the right mid-face softens.

See psi17.md for the full conjugate pair analysis and the face-region mapping of all three conjugate pairs.

## Conjugate Pair: psi17 + psi18

At matched weights, produces bilateral mid-face expression. See psi17.md for details.

## Symmetry

Asymmetric (score ~0.35). Right-lateralized by design. Mirrors psi17 across the sagittal plane.

## Practical Notes

- Not currently used in any expression recipe.
- Identical usage guidelines to psi17, but lateralized right.
- **Solo use** = right-sided mid-face tension.
- **Paired with psi17** = bilateral mid-face expression.
- No mesh artifacts at tested weights.

## Confidence

Low. Very subtle deformations. See psi17.md for caveats.
